About
Baristas often need to memorize a great deal of recipes. With multiple updates and additions of new drinks every year, it's no wonder that many could get confused with the proper way to make them.

Baristabook was created to enable baristas of all levels to craft drinks with a consistent quality. They can browse drink info to refresh their memory or save difficult ones in a personalized list for further study.

This simple yet functional app was developed by a barista from the bustling strip of Las Vegas, NV in Q1 2020. And although she enjoys making drinks and asking-flexing on her cashiers, 'Is that all you've got?' when she runs out of said drinks... (Only to be slammed dunked and be deceased thereafter...) She intended for this app to be more than just a tool that could be used for work - but as a ticket to even greater heights.
